🌁 The SF Fog Rule
SF in July is "Karl the Fog" season. Mornings start at 55–58°F with a marine layer before clearing by early afternoon. Always carry a layer — even if it feels mild at 11 AM. The Golden Gate Bridge, Ocean Beach, and the waterfront are significantly windier than inland neighborhoods like the Mission or Hayes Valley.
